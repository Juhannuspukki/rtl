Stay in Thailand for up to 14 months!

At RTL Scool, you can get a Non-immigrant Education visa, allowing you to stay in Thailand while studying the Thai language. Contact us to find out more, we will help with any paperwork!

Duration of ED visa and extensions

Basic studies

3 months

First, all students must attend the fundamental courses. The total duration of these courses is 3 months. After 3 months students will be taken to the Ministry of Education for a brief test to prove their skills.

Intermediate studies

5 months

If students pass the test and would like to continue their studies with an intermediate ED visa for 5 more months, RTL will file an extension for them.

Advanced studies

6 months

After the 5-month intermediate studies have been completed, students can apply for the advanced ED visa for 6 more months.
